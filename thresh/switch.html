SwitchActor

The SwitchActor is no longer understood. We recommend the BasicActor instead.

To use the SwitchActor implementation, load thresh.so and create an actor of type SwitchActor.

SwitchActor

In addition to the messages understood by the ThresholdActor, the SwitchActor understands the following messages:
  • Eval
  • SendAndEval
  • SetInputs
  • SetInput
  • SendData
  • SetTestVals
  • SetTestVal
  • SetGates
  • PrintAll
  • Add
  • Subtract
  • Init
  • Switch
  • RollOverflow
  • ResetOverflow
  • MultiEval
  • AssertGates
  • SetCombine
  • Case
  • IfTrue
  • IfFalse
  • AddMessage
